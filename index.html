Kanheri Caves

The Kanheri Caves, featuring 109 Buddha Viharas, were designed for Bhikshus and signify a large monastic settlement from the 1st century A.D. Each cell includes stone beds, water cisterns, and walkways. The caves are renowned for their exquisite architecture, beautifully displayed in both the interior and exterior.

Ellora Caves

Ellora, near Aurangabad in Maharashtra, comprises 34 monasteries and temples carved over 2 km into a basalt cliff. Dating from A.D. 600 to 1000, it showcases ancient India's civilization. This unique complex, dedicated to Buddhism, Hinduism, and Jainism, exemplifies the artistic brilliance and spirit of tolerance characteristic of ancient India.
